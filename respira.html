Terms, Privacy, and HIPAA Notice

Respira is a supportive monitoring tool for lung cancer patients and caregivers. It helps track symptoms, cough recordings, diary entries, medications, scans, and trends between visits. Respira does not diagnose disease, replace clinical judgment, or provide emergency care.

Most information you enter is stored locally on your device. Some optional AI features send limited text or files you choose to external AI services for explanation or summarization. Do not upload anything unless you are comfortable using those services.

Respira is not a HIPAA-covered provider, health plan, or clearinghouse. Unless you have a separate written agreement with a covered healthcare organization, Respira should not be treated as a HIPAA-compliant medical record system. Avoid storing highly sensitive information unless you accept that limitation.

You are responsible for deciding what to enter, upload, export, or share. If you believe you are having a medical emergency, call 911 or contact your care team immediately. If you are in emotional crisis, call or text 988 in the United States.

By continuing, you acknowledge that Respira is for education, organization, and wellness monitoring only; that AI-generated content can be incomplete or wrong; and that medical decisions must stay with licensed clinicians.
